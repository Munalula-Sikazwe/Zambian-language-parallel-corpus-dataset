Ono cinene ncakwaamba ayi bwikaalo bwanu bulyeelete kweendelana aMakani Abotu ayo aamba shakwe Klistu. Nabi ndesa mukumubona nabi ndabuula kwisa, ndyeelete kunyumfwa ayi mulimfwibo cakutatenkaana camuyeeyo womwi alimwi ayi mulacafwana kwiimikanina lushomo ndomucite muMakani Abotu.

Mwebo mwakapekwa coolwe cakusebensela Klistu, kuteshi kushomabo muli nguwe, sombi amukumupengela.

Ono nomusunikwi, kweelana ambomwalinga kunyumfwila ndindalingaanu, mulyeelete kwaamba ayi mwiindilishe kunoonyumfwila ciindi ici nceentabeteeko uko. Amutolelele kusebensa caboowa akututuma kwaamba ayi mumanishe mulimo wa lupulusho lwanu. 
Mukwiinga Lesa ngoosebensa muli ndimwe ayi muyande akucita shintu kweelana amuyeeyo wakwe.
Amucite shintu shoonse cakutatongooka alimwi akupikishanya. 
Amube bana bakwe Lesa basalalite, batabishi, batacite mulandu alimwi batacite kampenda. Pakati ka bantu batashomeki, bamucita shibi, mulyeelete kubala mbuli mimuni ili mwiculu, 
pakubalwiita maswi abuumi. Na mwacita boobo, ndaakuba asheebo shakunsangalasha ceebo ca ndimwe paBushiku ubo aakuboola Klistu, mukwiinga cilaakutondesha ayi ndakacicila nabi kusebensela ciliiwo.
Nabi na bulowa bwangu butootilwa anga ncipo ca cakunwa pantu pomwi acipo ico lushomo lwanu ncelutoobenga kuli Lesa, ndasangalala akusekelela pantu pomwi amwebo noonse. 
Aboobo amwebo noonse mulyeelete kusangalala akusekelela pantu pomwi anebo.
Na Mwaami mwiine wayanda, ntooyeeya kufwambaana kutuma Timoti kuli ndimwe kwaamba ayi moyo wangu ukale pakunyumfwa makani atooswa kumishaanu uko. 
Nshiciteewo aumwi muntu ngoonshoma ayi inga wamubikaako maano. 
Mukwiinga bamwi baanji balalisukamabo shintu shakulisangalasha beene kutaamba makani akwe Jesu Klistu. 
Mwebo noonse mulishi kale bubotu bwa milimo yakwe Timoti, mbuli mbondakasebensaakwe anga ngumwaana abaishi pakushimikila Makani Abotu. 
Paceebo ceeco, ntooshoma ayi ntoofwambaana kumutuma, ndaakubona mbweshi shikanyendele shintu. 
Ntooshoma ayi na Mwaami wayanda, anebo ndaakwisa uko coonoono.
Ono ndayeeya kwaamba ayi inga cacafwa kumubooshesha mukwabesu uyu Epafuloditasi, uyo musebenshima alimwi shilumambama muli Klistu. Lakwe ngumuntu ngomwakatuma ayi ancaafwe. 
Ono wamuyeeya abuumbi noonse alimwi wapenshekwa mumoyo mukwiinga mwakanyumfwa ayi wakalinga mulwashi. 
Ncakwiila wakalinga mulwashi alimwi lwakalingabo lufu, sombi Lesa wakamunyumfwila nkumbu. Kuteshibo lakwe eenka, sombi wakanyumfwila anebo kwaamba ayi nteeliki buumba pabuumba. 
Ntoolangilabo ciindi ndyeshi nkamutume kuli ndimwe, kwaamba ayi amwebo mukasangalale alimwi pakumubona. Lyalo anebo kulisukama kwangu ndyeshi kukaceye. 
Aboobo amumutambule camaansa obilo akusangalala ayi ngumukwabesu muMwaami. Bantu bamushobo wooyu boonse, amubalemeke. 
Wakaboolelabo pakalindi ceebo ca milimo yakwe Klistu. Wakaaba buumi bwakwe kwaamba ayi ancaafwe musheesho nshemwatakalinga kukonsha kuncafwa.
Ono cakweelaako nobamakwesu, kamusangalala muMwaami Jesu. Nshileemi kulemba shintu nshendakamulembelaawo kale mukwiinga maswi aya alaakumucafwa. 
Amucenjele abantu bacita shibiibi abo bali anga mbakabwa, abo bantu bacikakatisha kupalulwa. 
Swebo ndiswe twakapalulwa cancinencine sotutanjililwa aMushimu Usalashi mukulambila Lesa akusangalala muli Klistu Jesu. Aboobo tatushomi shapacishi ca panshi, sobwe. 
Teshi kwaamba ayi nebo ndabuula cintu cakusangalalilaamo. Na kuli muntu uyeeya ayi ulicite shintu sha mushobo uyo, nebo ndamwiindaawo. 
Nebo ndakapalulwa kaancite nsondo abushiku bomwi. Ndi mwine Isilaeli kukushalwa kwangu, mukowa wakwe Benjameni. Ndakaswa kubulowa bwiine bwa bamaEbulu. Ono kwaamba shakukonkela milawo ya baJuda nebo ndakalinga muFalisi. 
Ndakalinga shilunkutwe mukupensha coonde. Kwaamba shabululami buswa mukukonkela Milawo yeenka, ndakaboneka anga ngumuntu utacite shibiibi.
Nakubi boobo ceebo cakwe Klistu, shoonse nshendakalinga kubona ayi shilicite mpindu, ono ndacana ayi taaku mboshibete.

alimwi akucanika muli nguwe. Nshiliyeeyeli kwaamba ayi ndiluleme kwiinda mukukonkela Milawo, sombi bululami bwangu bulacanika kwiinda mukushoma Klistu. Bululami ubu buswa kuli Lesa bulapekwa kwiinda mulushomo. 
Ndisuni kwishiba Klistu ankusu shakubuka kwakwe kuswa kubafu, kukoshanyaakwe mumapensho akuba mbuli nguwe mulufu lwakwe.

Ndamulwiita lyoonse mankanda maanji alimwi ntekubooshaawo kaandila. Kuli bantu baanji batondesha mubuumi bwabo ayi mbalwani kumakani akwe Klistu pamucilingano. 
Kumeelelesho balaakunyonyoolwa mukwiinga lesa wabo nshintu shisangalasha mubili. Aboobo balo balasangalalila mushintu isho sheelete kubapa nsoni alimwi balayeeyabo shintu sha pacishi ca panshi. 
Swebo ncakwiila kumisheesu kwiine kwiine nkwiculu alimwi tulalangila Mupulushi wesu Mwaami Jesu Klistu kuswa nkomunya uko. 
Lakwe ulaakusandula mibili yesu ilefukite ya buntu kwaamba ayi ikabe mbuli mubili wakwe wa bulemu. Ulaakucita boobo munkusu shakwe isho shikoma shintu shoonse akushilela.
Ono mwebo nobamakwesu, nomusunikwi alimwi ndamuyeeya abuumbi. Mwebo mulansangalasha. Paceebo ceeco ndatangala cankusu ceebo ca ndimwe. Anu ubo nobamakwesu mbomweelete kuyuma muMwaami.
