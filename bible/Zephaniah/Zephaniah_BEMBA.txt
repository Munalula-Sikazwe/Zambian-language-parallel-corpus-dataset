 Ishiwi lyakwa Yawe ilyaishile kuli Sefania mwana Kushi uwali mwana Gedalia, na Gedalia nao ali ni mwana Amaria, uwali mwana wa mfumu Hesekia, pa nshita Yosia umwana wa mfumu Amoni ateekele icalo ca Yuda.
Yawe alisosele ati,
nkalofya abantu bonse,
“Ine nkakanda abena Yuda
Nkalofya bonse abaya pa mitenge
Nkalofya bonse abansangukila,
Ubushiku bwakwa Yawe nabupalama,
Yawe aletila ati, “Pali ubo bushiku ubwe lambo lyandi,
Pali ubo ubushiku nkakanda bonse abaciluka pa mwinshi ilyo baleingila,
Yawe asosa ati,
Ilyo mukomfwa ifi fintu mukapumepo imisowa
Pali iyo inshita ine nkabuula ifisaniko,
Ala ifyuma fyabo fikatapwa,
Ubushiku ubukalamba ubwakwa Yawe nabupalama,
Bulya bushiku bukaba ubushiku bwabukali,
Ubushiku ubwakulisha ipenga ne nkuuta sha bulwi,
Ine nkaletela abantu ubulanda,
Nangu ni silifa wabo na golide wabo takakwate maka yakubapusushako,
Longaneni pamo, longaneni pamo,
ilyo inshita iyapimwa tailafika,
Fwayeni Yawe imwe bonse mwe baicefya pano calo,
 Mu Gaza tamwakashale umuntu nangu umo,
Mwashama imwe mwe bekala mu lulamba lwa bemba,
Icalo ca mu lulamba lwa bemba,
Cikaba cuma ca bakashalapo aba mu ŋanda yakwa Yuda.
 Nalyumfwa insele shakwa Moabu,
 “Eico, napalifye ine ne Mumi,”
Aya e malipilo ya cilumba cabo,
Yawe akaba uwakutiinya
 Na imwe mwe bena Kushi,
 Yawe akatambalika ukuboko kwakwe lwa ku kapinda ka ku kuso pakonaula Asiria,
Imikuni ya mpaanga, imbushi ne ŋombe efikalalalamo
Uyu musumba uwaleikalila umutelelwe,
Walishama we musumba uwatalama,
Taumfwa kabili taupokelela na mafunde nakalya.
Intungulushi ishabamo ni nkalamo ishilebuluma,
Bakasesema bauko ni bacilande lande,
Lelo Yawe uwaba mu musumba, acitamofye ifyabololoke,
Yawe aletila ati,
Naletila nati, ‘Nomba abantu bakulantiina,
Eico Yawe aletila ati, “Cisuma mulolelefye.
Elyo nkapilibula imisosele ya bantu ba mishobo yonse,
Abantu bandi abampepa,
Mu bushiku bulya, we musumba, tawakabe na nsoni
Lelo muli iwe nkashamofye abantu abafuuka, kabili abanakilila,
 Abakashalamo mu bena Israeli tabakalecita ulufyengo,
Imba ne nsansa we Mwana mwanakashi wa Sioni.
Yawe nafumyapo ifyo akupingwile,
Mu bushiku bulya bakasosa mu Yerusalemu abati,
Yawe Lesa obe ali mukati kobe,
nga pa nshita ya nshikunkulu iyasontwa.”
Mona inshita ikesa ilyo nkemina bonse abakucusha,
Inshita ikesa ilyo nkamulonganya,
