Chimwenu chaObadiya.
Tiya, nakukeheshi dehi mukachi kanyunza,
Eyi wáshakama munyaji yamwilola,
Hela wukujindama hewulu neyi mbuulu,
Eyi wakisika dehi!
Esawu O chamusandolawu dehi!
Akwenu ejima watiyaŋeni nawu anakuhaŋi dehi ndo-o nikuŋinza.
Yehova nindi,
Eyi Temani, ayilobu jeyi akatiya nyima kotu,
Nsonyi jakakubuta wi-i
Hefuku diwemeni hahalehi nayena,
Bayi wutala ifuku damanakweyi mwifuku dakumonayi kukalaku,
Bayi wiŋila muchisu champwembu yawantu ami mwifuku dakuhalwawuku;
Bayi mwimana kumaakenu nakuyijikilila antu indi akupulukaku;
Muloŋa ifuku daYehova didi kwakwihi,
Muloŋa chimunanwi enu hampidi yami yajila,
Ilaŋa kumpidi yaZiyona kwakekala ákapuluka,
Akwamutaŋa waYakoba akekala kesi,
Awa akukabeta kakuchidiilu akaheta mpidi yaEsawu,
Wunkoli wanidi izaza dawanyana kaIsarela ekala mukachi kawaKenani,
Akwakwamwina akakanduka mpidi yaZiyona
