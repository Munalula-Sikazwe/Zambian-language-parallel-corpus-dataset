Nceeci cilengaano ca Obadiya. Mboobu mbwaamba Mwami Leza azyacisi ca Edomu.
Bona, ndiyoocita kuti ube musyoonto akati kamisyobo, uyoosampaulwa kapati.
Kulisumpula kwamoyo wako kwakakucenga,
Nikuba kuti uuluke mujulu mbuli sikwaze
Nikwali kuti babbi bakakusikila, nikwali kuti bafumpi bakakuboolela masiku,
Nywebo nolunyungu lwa Isau ziyobwedo zyanu zisisidwe ziyoosaalwa!
Bantu boonse mbomumvwana limwi bayoomutandila kumunyinza,
Mwami Leza ulati, “Mubuzuba oobo,
Basilumamba banu, nobamu Temani bayooyoowa!
Akaambo kamanyongwe ngomwacita kumwanookwanu Jakobo,
Mubuzuba oobo mwakaide kudankamana kakuli bazwakule babweza lubono,
Tamweelede kulekelezya mwanookwanu mubuzuba bwamapenzi
Tamweelede kwiinda mumilyango yabantu bangu mubuzuba bwamapenzi aabo,
Tamweelede kubayubilila mumaswaanganino aamigwagwa
“Buzuba bwa Mwami Leza buli aafwiifwi kuti abeteke misyobo yoonse,
Mbubonya mbuli mbumwakali kunywida acilundu cangu cisalala, mbubonya oobo bamasi bayoonywida mpawo cakutaleka,
“Pele acilundu ca Ziyoni kuyooba nkamu yabantu batijide,
Bantu bamusyobo wa Jakobo, awa Josefa bayooba mbuli mulilo uuvwilima,
“Bajuda bayoozwida ku Negevu kumusanza bayookkala muzilundu zya Isau,
