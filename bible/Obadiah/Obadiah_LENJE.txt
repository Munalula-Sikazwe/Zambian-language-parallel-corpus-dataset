Ishi nshesheeshi Mwaami Lesa nshaakayubulwita Obadiya anga muciloto. Makani aya atokwaamba pabene Edomu.
Mwaami Lesa utokwaambila bene Edomu ayi,
Kulisumpula kwa myoyo yanu kwamubeca.
Nabi muuluke akuya mwiculu abuumbi anga ngumumbankwashi,
“Ono na beepi bamupolela mashiku,
Sombi mwebo nobamulunyungu lwakwe Esau,
Babyaanu bamucenjeeka,
Mwaami Lesa utokwaamba ayi,
Bashilumamba banu ba mucishi ca Temani balaakuloonda abuumbi,
“Ceebo ca shimfulunganya nshemwakacita kubamakwabanu bamulunyungu lwakwe Jakobo,
Pabushiku ubo mwakeenkemanabo palubasu
Temwakalinga kweelete kukondwa
Temwakalinga kweelete kwinjila mundabala inene ya bantu bangu
Anu temwakalinga kweeletewo kwiimikana pamampambaanwa anshila
“Bushiku buli pafwaafwi ubo nebo neMwaami Lesa,
Bweenka mbuli mbomwakanwa munkomeshi ya bukali pakalundu kangu kasalalite,
Mwaami Lesa waamba ayi,
Bamulunyungu lwakwe Jakobo balaakuba mulilo,
“Bantu ba kumalenge a cishi ca Juda,
Likoto lya bene Isilaeli bakaloboka,
Beshikupulusha balaakutanta pakalundu ka Siyoni
