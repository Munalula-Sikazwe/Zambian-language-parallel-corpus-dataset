Ndime Judi musebenshi wakwe Jesu Klistu alimwi mukwabo Jemusi.
Lesa amunyumfwile nkumbu shiteeli akumupa luumuno lutamaani alimwi akukomenya lusuno lwanu.
Nomusunikwi, ndakalinga kusuni abuumbi kumulembela nkalata kwaamba ayi musansulwite lupulusho ndotucite swebo sobashomi soonse. Sombi ndakabona kwaamba ayi inga cabota kumulembela nkalata yakumuyumya ayi mulame camaansa obilo lushomo ulo Lesa ndwaakapa bantu bakwe linkanda lyomwibo. 
Ndakacita boobo mukwiinga bantu bamwi batasuni kucita kuyanda kwakwe Lesa bakenjila mulikoto lyanu cabushishi. Bantu aba balafulunganya lwaanguluko ndotucite kwaamba ayi bacaninemo nshila yakunoocita shintu shilensha nsoni. Peculu lya waawo balakaka Jesu Klistu Mufundishi alimwi ngoMwaami wesu eenkabo. Shamulandu wa bantu aba shakalembwabo kale kale muMalembo akwe Lesa.
Ono ndisuni kumwiibalusha makani amwi, nakubi kwaamba ayi mulyeeshi kale. Mwaami wakafuna bene Isilaeli kuswa muIjipiti, sombi kunembo wakanyonyoola baabo boonse batakwe kumushoma. 
Amwiibaluke baanjelo balya batakwe kukondwa kunooli mumisena ya nkusu nshebakapekwa, sombi bakashiya musena wakabapa Lesa kwiculu. Lesa wakabaanga ncetani shitanyonyooki alimwi ulibasungite mumusena wa munshinshe kushikila paBushiku bunene ndyeshi akaboomboloshe akubapa mulandu. 
Amwiibaluke andabala ya Sodoma aGomola amandabala akalinga pafwaafwi. Bantu ba mumandabala aya abalo bakatalika kucita bufuule alimwi akucitana shintu shilensha nsoni. Lesa wakabapa mulandu wa mulilo utamaani kwaamba ayi cibe citondesho kubantu boonse.
Sombi bantu aba balacita mbomunya ubo. Kwiinda mukukonkela shiloto shabo, balabisha mibili yabo. Balakaka nkusu shakwe Lesa alimwi akucobola baanjelo bakwe balemekwa. 
Nabi munene wa baanjelo Mikayelo, ndyaakalinga kupikishanya aMubiibi pacikani cakubwesa mutuntu wakwe Mose, taakwe kupa mulandu Mubiibi ulya camucobwe, sombi wakaambabo ayi, “Mwaami Lesa akupe mulandu!” 
Sombi bantu aba balacobola cintu cili coonse ncebatanyumfwishi. Ono shintu isho nshebeshi kabotu mukusebensesha mibo yabunyama, nsheshintu sheshi shikabanyonyoole. 
Mapensho kuli mbabo! Bakonkela nshila yakwe Kaini. Bweenka bwakacita Balamu, abalo balawita mushibiibi ceebo ca kusuna bunene maali. Balakaka kunyumfwila Lesa bweenka bwakacita Kola, aboobo balaakunyonyoolwa abalo. 
Bali anga ngamatombe mushakulya shapakusekelela mubwanabwankashi. Mbantu balyabi, bayeeyabo shabo beene. Bali anga ngamakumbi atolaanishikwabo amuuwo, sombi taaleti mfula. Alimwi bali anga nshisamu shitacati shisepo na cashikila ciindi, nabi shisamu shakashulwa akuyuma cakumaninina. 
Micito yabo ilensha nsoni ili anga ndyoofu amatombe, shicaala muloombe lwa lweenga ceebo ca maaba. Balibo anga ninyenyeenshi shaluba moshiinda. Paceebo ceeco, Lesa wakababambila musena mumunshinshe utamaani.
Mbobaaba mbwaakalaluka Enoki, wasanu abobilo mubushalwe kuswa kuli Adamu, ndyakaamba ayi, “Amubone, Mwaami ulaakwisa abaanjelo bakwe basalalite bali shuulu ashuulu. 
Ulaakwisa mukoombolosha akupa mulandu bantu boonse, batanyumfwili Lesa, pashibiibi nshebakacita mububi bwabo, mukutalulama kwabo alimwi aceebo ca maswi abiibi bamucita shibi aba ngobakacobola Lesa.” 
Bantu mbomunya aba mbeshi kutongooka lyoonse akubekesha bamwi shilubo, balakonkela shiyanda mibili yabo shibiibi. Balaamba cakulisumpula alimwi akubotesha baabo bacite buboni ayi babapeewo.
Sombi nomusunikwi, amwiibaluke maswi akamulwiita batumwi ba Mwaami Jesu Klistu kale kale. 
Bakamulwiita ayi, “Munshiku shakweelaako, kulaakuba bantu beshibakanoomuseka akukonkela shibiibi shiyanda mibili yabo.” 
Bantu aba mbobapansaanya babyaabo. Balakonkelabo shintu sha mucishi ca panshi nshebeshi alimwi tabacite Mushimu Usalashi. 
Sombi mwebo nomusunikwi, amuliyaake mulushomo lwanu lusalalite abuumbi. Munoopaila munkusu sha Mushimu Usalashi. 
Amwikale mulusuno lwakwe Lesa pakunoolindilila buumi butamaani Mwaami wesu Jesu Klistu mbweshi akatupe munkumbu shakwe.
Amunyumfwile nkumbu bacitonsha. 
Bamwi amubayokole mumulilo utamaani akubapulusha. Kuli bamwi beendeleshekwa ashiyanda mibili yabo shibiibi, amubanyumfwile nkumbu. Sombi mulyeelete kucenjela kwaamba ayi mutakoweli abuumi bwa bubi.
Atulumbaishe Lesa, uyo eenkabo ukonsha kumulama kwaamba ayi mutabishi alimwi akumushisha kooko nkwaabete mubulemu bwakwe kamutacite kampenda nakaceyi alimwi kamusangalete abuumbi. 
Kwiinda muli Jesu Klistu Mwaami wesu, atulumbaishe Lesa eenkabo Mupulushi wesu ayi abe abulemu, bwaami alimwi ankusu shakweendelesha kuswa kumatalikilo, bulya alimwi anshiiku shoonse shitoosa. Ameni.
