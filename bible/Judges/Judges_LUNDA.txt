Ami Yuda, nduŋu yaYesu Kristu, manakwaYakoba, nidi nakusoneka mukanda wenwenu atambikewa, akeŋewa mudi Nzambi Tata, ánahembeluwu mudi Yesu Kristu.
Luwi lwikali nanenu, nikuwunda nikukeŋa kuyivulili.
Enu akwetu twakeŋa, nakeŋa nami nidikwatishi kuyisonekela nsaŋu yawamwinu wetu wamawaninu, ilaŋa hinukutiya kumuchima nami chiŋa niyisonekeli mazu akuyikonkomwena, nami lwililenu naŋovu chikuhwelelu chenu chenkeluwu akwakujila kamu hohu chahayanyaka yejima.
Muloŋa kudi antu amakwawu anadikokelumu hambadi, wowu atoŋwelawu dehi chachikulu nawu akayihisha. Hiyantu adiwulaŋa Nzambi, abalumunaŋa luwi lwaNzambi yetu lwikali kubajama, nakudikala Mwanta wetu Chinyaweji, yowu Yesu Kristu.
Hela mweluka dehi yuma yejima chikupu, nakeŋi kuyanukisha nami, Mwanta, chapulwishiliyi munza windi mwituŋa daEjipitu, nkumininaku hakujilumuna antu abulili kukuhwelela.
 Niaŋelu abulili kuhemba wanta wawu, hiyakufuma mwaawu mwashakaminaŋawu, nayihembi mumaleŋi ahayanyaka mumwidima wiyila bwi-i, nayiswekeli kusompesha kwefuku odu deneni.
 ASodoma naGomora, nanyikala yanyeŋumukiluku, adumbilili muwujila nakwila yuma yabula kuya mwayileŋeleluwu, chamwenuwu kubabesha kwakesi kahayanyaka, anekali dehi akutalilahu.
Mwomumu niwenawa nawa atamishaŋa nyijimba yawu namwatoŋojokaŋawu, adiwulaŋa wanta, atenaŋa akwakulema chatama.
Hakwila mukulumpi wawaŋelu Mikeli, hafwiliyi ntadi nadiyabolu hansaŋu yamujimba waMosi, hatwesheli kumuhosha chatama nakumwinka muloŋaku; wakuminini dakwila nindi, MWANTA akuhosholi.
Hakwila wena atenaŋa yuma yejima yabula kwilukawu chatama; hiyakuditokesha nayuma yamwaya chisemwa yeluka nianyama abulaŋa kutoŋojoka.
Oyu yihuŋu yakamonawu! Muloŋa ayili munjila yaKena; alundumukilili mwaluwili Balami nakulonda wulooku; anoŋeleli muntadi yaKora.
Wenawa himalola akuhukena akadi kumwekana hatooka mumawijenu akukeŋaŋana, hadyaŋawu nanenu; hiyatubiŋa adidiishaŋa awenawu chakadi woma; himavu akadi menji alelokaŋa nampepela; hinyitondu yahanoŋa yakadi nyikabu, yafwa kayedi, yabwitika nikunyiji;
himankanda akukaluŋalwiji azuwa, atupukaŋa mafulolu ansonyi yawu aweni; hitutumbwa twahitahitaŋa, twanaswekeluwu mwidima wiyila bwi-i wahayanyaka.
Wenawa diwu aprofweteleluwu kudi Enoki, wamuchitanu nachiyedi wakufumisha kudi Adama, nindi, Tiyenu, MWANTA wakenza namakumi amakombakaji awantu indi ajila,
nakwila mudimu wakusompesha nakukonkolola ejima adiwulaŋa Nzambi, nanyidimu yawu yakudiwula Nzambi yakoŋeluwu chakudiwula Nzambi, nayuma yakala yejima yamuhosheluwu akwanshidi adiwulaŋa Nzambi.
Wenawa hiyakwakunyinyita, hiyakwakutoba, endaŋa mwaya kufwila kwawu kwatama. Tunwa twawu twahoshaŋa mazu atiyakana akudinka wanta; alemeshaŋa antu kumesu kulonda kudiilaku yuma.
Ilaŋa enu akwetu twakeŋa, anukenu mazu ahosheluwu dehi apostolu jaMwanta wetu Yesu Kristu,
ayilejeluwu nawu, Kunyaka yakukuminina kwakekala akwakuseha, akendaŋa mwaya kufwila kwawu kwakudiwula Nzambi.
Wenawa diwu aambokaŋa, ekali yendu; hiyantu elaŋa mwaya mujimba hohu, hiyakweti Spirituku.
Hakwila enu akwetu twakeŋa, dituŋenu mukukuhwelela kwenu kwabadika kujila, nakulombela mudi Spiritu Wajila.
Dihembenu kulonda mushakami mukukeŋa kwaNzambi, nakutalila luwi lwaMwanta wetu Yesu Kristu lwashika kuwumi wahayanyaka.

amakwawu yihandishenu chakuyihukula mukesi; amakwawu yitiyilenu luwi nawoma, nakuhela niihina dakuvwala dinasampukili majilu achisemwa chaheseki.
