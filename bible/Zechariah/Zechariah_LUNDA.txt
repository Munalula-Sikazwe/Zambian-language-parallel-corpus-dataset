Dena ifuku odu Yehova wakalwila akwakushakama muYerusalema. Dena ifuku odu wabula ŋovu mukachi kawu wakekala neyi Davidi, mutaŋa waDavidi wakekala neyi Nzambi, neyi kaŋelu kaYehova kadi kumesu awu.
Dena ifuku odu nakakeŋa kujilumuna nyunza yejima yakenza nakulwa naYerusalema.
Hamutaŋa waDavidi nihadi akwakushakama muYerusalema nakechilahu Spiritu waluwi niwakulembelela; akatala hadami alokweluwu. Akamudila neyi chadilaŋayi muntu mwanindi wasemuka kankawindi, akamutiyila kulula kumuchima, neyi chatiyaŋayi muntu kulula hadi wedi windi.
Dena ifuku odu muYerusalema mwakekala kudila kwasweja nankashi, neyi kudila kwadililuwu kuHadadirimoni muchinekineki chaMegido.
Ituŋa dakadila, chisaka nahachu, chisaka nahachu; chisaka chamutaŋa waDavidi nahachu, aŋodijawu nahawu; chisaka chamutaŋa waNatani nahachu, aŋodijawu nahawu;
Dena ifuku odu akajikwila mutaŋa waDavidi, niakwakushakama muYerusalema, kaseloki kakutookesha nachu nshidi namajilu.
Yehova Wamazaza nindi, Dena ifuku odu mwituŋa nakajiyishamu majina awankishi jakutuŋa, hiyakayanukaŋa nawa cheŋuku. Mwituŋa nakafumishamu nawa atuprofwetu nispiritu wamajilu.
Akelaŋa chakelayi muntu nindi aprofweti, tata yindi namama yindi amusema akamwila nawu, Eyi hiwukuhandaku, muloŋa wudi nakuhosha yuma yakutwamba mwijina daYehova. Chakamwekeshayi yijindilu tata yindi namama yindi amusema akamupwika nzwa-a.
Dena ifuku odu atuprofwetu ejima chakaprofwetawu akatiya nsonyi yayimwenu yawu, iwu nachindi, iwu nachindi; hiyakavwala nawa mahina awuvuji kulonda kudimbana nachuku.
Ilaŋa yena wakela nindi, Ami hinikaprofwetuku, nidi mukwakudima kwami; ami kwami ankwata dehi wuduŋu kufuma kuwansi wami.
Muntu wumu wakamwihula nindi, Indi iyi yitu yidi mukachi kamakaseyi yanka? Yena wakaakula nindi, Yoyu yantapiluwu mwitala dawamabwambu jami.
