 Hatachi Nzambi waleŋeli nimawulu nimaseki.
Maseki adiŋi akadi nimwayawu, adiŋi amukunkulu; hewulu denyiŋi hadiŋi mwidima. Spiritu WaNzambi wadiŋi nakuhitahita hewulu damenji.
Nzambi wahosheli nindi, kwikali chejeji. Kwikala nichejeji to-o.
Nzambi wamweni chejeji nindi chinawahi; Nzambi waambwili chejeji namwidima.
Nzambi watúmbili chejeji nindi, Mwaana, mwidima wawutúmbili nindi, Wufuku. Kwila nimelela, kwila nintetemena, ifuku dimu.
Nzambi wahosheli nindi, Mukachi kamenji mwikali luzwizu, lwambuli menji namenji.
Nzambi welili luzwizu, waambwili menji, awa kwishina daluzwizu, awa kwiwulu daluzwizu; chila nikwila.
Nzambi watúmbili luzwizu ijina, nindi, Iwulu. Kwila nimelela, kwila nintetemena, ifuku damuchiyedi.
Nzambi wahosheli nindi, Menji adi mwishina dewulu apompi kwawu kumu, hawuma hamwekani. Chila nikwila.
Hana hawuma Nzambi watumbiluhu ijina, nindi, Maseki; kwapompeleli menji watumbiluku ijina, nindi, Tuluŋalwiji; Nzambi wamweni nindi chinawahi.
Nzambi wahosheli nindi, Hamaseki hameni nimatahu, niyisakola yasoŋaŋa mbutu, ninyitondu yasoŋaŋa nyikabu yikweti nyisoka yayu, iwu namwawu, iwu namwawu, hamaseki; chila nikwila.
Hamaseki hamena nimatahu niyisakola yasoŋaŋa mbutu, iyi namwayu, iyi namwayu, ninyitondu yasoŋaŋa nyikabu yikweti nyisoka yayu, iwu namwawu, iwu namwawu. Nzambi wayimweni nindi yayiwahi.
Kwila nimelela, kwila nintetemena, ifuku damuchisatu.
Nzambi wahosheli nindi, Muluzwizu mwikali yuma yakutoŋeka, kulonda kwambula nimwaana niwufuku; yakekali yinjikijilu, yayilaaka, yamafuku, niyanyaka;
yakekali yakutoŋeka muluzwizu, yakatoŋeki hanu hamaseki to-o; chila nikwila.
Nzambi welili yuma yamaneni yakutoŋeka yiyedi; chasweja kutoha chakekali mwanta wahamwaana, chakehaku chakekali mwanta wahawufuku; welili nitutumbwa nawa.
Nzambi washili yumiyi muluzwizu kulonda yakatoŋekaŋa chejeji hamaseki to-o,
yakekali nawanta niwamwaana niwawufuku, nakwambula chejeji namwidima. Nzambi wachimweni nindi chinawahi.
Kwila nimelela, kwila nintetemena, ifuku damuchiwana.
Nzambi wahosheli nindi, mumenji mukumini dakulumbama yendu yayuma yendaŋa, yikweti wumi, niatuda atuukaŋa hewulu damaseki hejima hahita luzwizu.
Nzambi waleŋeli anyama amaneni amumenji, niyuma yejima yendaŋa yikweti wumi, yalumbama mumenji, hanyichidi, hanyichidi, niatuda akweti mpuula, hamuchidi, hamuchidi. Nzambi wachimweni nindi chinawahi.
Nzambi wayikiswilili nkisu, nindi, Semununenu muvuleŋa, inzeshenu mumenji amutuluŋalwiji niatuda avuleŋa hamaseki.
Kwila nimelela, kwila nintetemena, ifuku damuchitanu.
Nzambi wahosheli nindi, Hamaseki hediki yuma yikweti wumi, hamuchidi, hamuchidi, niayimuna, niyuma yazalalaŋa, nianyama amumpata hamuchidi, hamuchidi; chila nikwila.
Nzambi welili nianyama amumpata hamuchidi, hamuchidi, niayimuna hamuchidi, hamuchidi, niyuma yejima yazalalaŋa hamaseki hamuchidi, hamuchidi. Nzambi wachimweni nindi chinawahi.
Nzambi wahosheli nindi, Twilenu muntu wudi neyi chitwekaletu, adifwani nanetu, akekali mwanta niwawanshi amukaluŋalwiji, niwawatuda amuluzwizu, niwawanyama, niweseki dejima, niwayuma yazalalaŋa hamaseki yejima.
Nzambi yoleŋa nimuntu neyi chekalayi yomweni, chochekalayi Nzambi dichi chamuleŋeliyi; wayileŋeli niiyala nimumbanda.
Nzambi wayikiswilili nkisu, Nzambi hakuyila nindi, Semununenu, muvuleŋa, muwanini heseki, mudovwahishi; ikalenu anyanta niawanshi amukaluŋalwiji, niawatuda amuluzwizu, niayuma yayumi yejima yendaŋa hamaseki.
