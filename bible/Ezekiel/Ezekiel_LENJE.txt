Kumatalikilo Lesa wakalenga liculu acishi ca panshi. 
Ono cishi taaku mbocakalingaawo ceebo temwakalinga shintu. Kwakalingabo munshinshe peculu lya maanshi aanji. Mushimu wakwe Lesa wakalinga kweendeendabo peculu lya maanshi. 
Mpeeke Lesa wakaamba ayi, “Akube mumuni.” Lyalo mumuni wakabaako. 
Lesa wakabona kwaamba ayi mumuni ngubotu, lyalo wakapansaanya mumuni amunshinshe. 
Mpeeke Lesa wakatumba mumuni ayi, “Malumwi,” awalo munshinshe ayi, “Mashiku.” Lyalo kwakaba macolesha amacuunsa, ubu bwakaba mbushiku butaanshi.
 Ono Lesa wakaamba ayi, “Akube musena ufongomene kwiculu kwaamba ayi upansaanye maanshi.” 
Aboobo Lesa wakalenga musena ufongomene uyo akupansaanya maanshi ayo apanshi lya nguwo alimwi aayo ali peculu lya nguwo. Mpeeke kwakaba bweenka ubo. 
Lesa wakatumba musena uyo ayi, “Liculu.” Lyalo kwakaba macolesha amacuunsa, ubu bwakaba mbushiku bwabili.
Kuswawaawo Lesa wakaamba ayi, “Maanshi ali panshi lya musena ufongomene uyo abungane mumusena womwi kwaamba ayi bulongo buyumu buboneke.” Mpeeke kwakaba bweenka ubo. 
Lesa wakatumba bulongo buyumu ubo ayi, “Inshi,” aalo maanshi akabunganishikwa pantu pomwi ayi, “Lweenge.” Mpeeke Lesa wakabona kwaamba ayi shintu ishi nshibotu. 
Kuswawaawo Lesa wakaamba ayi, “Inshi aimene shishango isho shicata nseke, ashisamu shicata shisepo shicite mikoli kweelana amushobo wa comwi acomwi.” Mpeeke kwakaba bweenka ubo. 
Aboobo inshi yakamena shishango isho shicata nseke, ashisamu shicata shisepo shicite mikoli kweelana amushobo wa comwi acomwi. Ono Lesa wakabona kwaamba ayi shintu ishi nshibotu. 
Lyalo kwakaba macolesha amacuunsa, ubu bwakaba mbushiku bwatatu.
Kuswawaawo Lesa wakaamba ayi, “Akube mimuni mumusena ufongomene kwiculu ayi ipansaanye malumwi amashiku, alimwi akwaamba ayi shibe shitondesho sha shiindi, inshiku amyaaka. 
Mimuni iyo aibe mumusena ufongomene uyo kwaamba ayi inoomunikila cishi ca panshi.” Mpeeke kwakaba bweenka ubo. 
Lesa wakalenga mimuni inene yobilo, lisuba kwaamba ayi linokweendelesha malumwi, amweenshi ayi unokweendelesha mashiku. Alimwi wakalenga anyenyeenshi. 
Lesa wakashibamba mumusena ufongomene uyo kwaamba ayi shinoomunikila cishi ca panshi, 
mukweendelesha malumwi amashiku, alimwi akupansaanya mumuni amunshinshe. Mpeeke Lesa wakabona kwaamba ayi shintu ishi nshibotu. 
Mpeeke kwakaba macolesha alimwi amacuunsa, ubu bwakaba mbushiku bwane. 
Kuswawaawo Lesa wakaamba ayi, “Mumaanshi amube shintu shuumi shipusenepusene, alimwi abayuni bauluka mwiculu.” 
Aboobo Lesa wakalenga banyama banene ba mulweenge ashintu shoonse shuumi, alimwi sheenda isho shakafula mumaanshi kweelana amushobo wa comwi acomwi, abayuni bauluka abalo kweelana amushobo wa mbabo. Mpeeke Lesa wakabona kwaamba ayi shintu ishi nshibotu. 
Lyalo Lesa wakashipa coolwe akwaamba ayi, “Amushalane, mufule alimwi musushe maanshi a mulweenge, abalo bayuni bafule mucishi ca panshi.” 
Mpeeke kwakaba macolesha amacuunsa, ubu bwakaba mbushiku bwasanu.
Kuswawaawo Lesa wakaamba ayi, “Painshi apabe shintu shuumi comwi acomwi kweelana amushobo wa ncico. Apabe banyama bafubwa, shilongolo shinene atuniini sheenda panshi abanyama bamuluundu, womwi awomwi kweelana amushobo wakwe.” Mpeeke kwakaba bweenka ubo. 
Lesa wakalenga banyama bamuluundu, womwi awomwi kweelana amushobo wakwe, banyama bafubwa kweelana amishobo yabo ashilongolo shinene atuniini sheenda panshi kweelana amishobo ya nshisho. Mpeeke Lesa wakabona kwaamba ayi shintu ishi nshibotu.
Ono Lesa wakaamba ayi, “Atulenge muntu mucikoshanyo cesu, anooboneka anga ndiswe. Bakanokweendelesha nswi sha mumaanshi, bayuni bauluka, banyama bafubwa, banyama bamuluundu ashilongolo shinene atuniini sheenda panshi shoonse.”
 Aboobo Lesa wakalenga muntu,
Lyalo Lesa wakabapa coolwe akubaambila ayi, “Amushalane mufule, akususha cishi ca panshi. Amweendeleshe inswi sha mumaanshi, bayuni bauluka ashilongolo shinene atuniini sheenda panshi shoonse.”
